Welcome

Our Concept

What started as a schoolproject became reality after 4 months of hard work. We like to call it Project:LUX, but as of now LUX is one of the most exclusive conceptstores ever created. A team of four awesome people started thinking of an idea, a need that every artist had. A question an artist poses to himself almost everyday: what am I gonna make next? Inspiration isn't perpetual. But light is. And light creates creativity. Light creates colors and shapes reality. The lack of light, the so-called darkness could feel like an anti-power. But if combined together light and darkness can become a harmonious duo. We tried creating that magic. Our concept exists of a parallelogram in 3D with a smaller accesselement.

Enter the block

When entering our store you will be enlightened, literally! Let light be your guide through a space full of subtle lighting and luminous elements. Not only is LUX a store that provides the full experience. We also bring with us solutions and many more. Our biggest assortiment consists of beamers, lighting facilities and many more. We specialize in presenting and testing on the moment itself and grant the viewer a unique look behind the scenes. Testing things is toppriority since only then you can be assured to have had the most correct input as possible. We also want to hear from your feedback whenever you like.
